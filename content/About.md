## About Me

I am a Technical Artist specializing in stylized rendering for games and
interactive experiences. My work bridges visual quality and production
stability by turning rendering ideas into practical, reusable workflows.

I focus on custom shading models, look development, and robust art pipelines
across Unreal and Unity. I enjoy translating art direction into clear technical
systems that artists can iterate on quickly.

I am particularly interested in roles where I can contribute to rendering
quality, performance optimization, and cross-discipline collaboration.
